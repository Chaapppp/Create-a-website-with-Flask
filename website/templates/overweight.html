Overweight

Description

Overweight individuals have a BMI between 25 and 29.9, indicating excess body weight and an increased risk of various health problems.

Causes

  • Consuming more calories than needed.
  • Insufficient physical activity.

    • Risks

  • Increased risk of heart disease.
  • Increased risk of diabetes.
  • Increased risk of stroke.
  • Increased risk of certain cancers.

    • Recommendations

  • Aim for gradual weight loss.
  • Adjust dietary habits.
  • Increase physical activity.

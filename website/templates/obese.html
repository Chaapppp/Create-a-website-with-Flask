Obese

Description

BMI exceeding 30 indicates obesity, which significantly elevates the risk of developing various health conditions.

Causes

  • Poor dietary habits.
  • Lack of physical activity.
  • Sedentary lifestyle.

    • Risks

  • High risk of heart disease.
  • High risk of diabetes.
  • High risk of stroke.
  • High risk of certain cancers.

    • Recommendations

  • Seek professional guidance for weight reduction.
  • Adopt a well-balanced diet.
  • Increase physical activity.
  • Consider medical intervention under supervision.

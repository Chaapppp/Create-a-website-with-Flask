Normal

Description

Normal weight indicates a BMI between 18.5 and 24.9, reflecting a healthy state for the majority of individuals. Those with normal weight usually enjoy good health with a lower risk of various diseases.

Causes

  • Balanced and varied food intake.
  • Regular physical activity.

    • Risks

  • Lower risk of heart disease.
  • Lower risk of stroke.
  • Lower risk of diabetes.
  • Lower risk of certain cancers.
  • Lower risk of osteoarthritis.

    • Recommendations

  • Maintain weight within the normal range.
  • Consume a diverse and balanced diet.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Avoid high-fat, high-sugar, and high-sodium foods.
  • Ensure adequate hydration and quality sleep.
